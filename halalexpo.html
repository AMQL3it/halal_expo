About Halal Expo

Make Bangladesh a central country in the $10 trillion halal market

Connecting Continents Through Halal Trade

The 1st International Dhaka Halal Expo 2026 is organized under the auspices of the Presidency of Bangladesh, in collaboration with SMIIC and ICDT. It stands as the largest international trade fair for the halal economy in the region.

With featured areas focusing on NOV (Natural, Organic, Vegan), Private Label, and SEFPEX (Aquaculture), we provide a unified platform for industry leaders to showcase innovation and tradition.
