About Bangladesh Halal Expo

To make Bangladesh one of the leading halal economies in the world

Connecting Continents Through the Global Halal Economy

Bangladesh Halal Expo 2026 stands as a trusted meeting point of the global halal economy, bringing together international exhibitors, buyers, innovators, and policymakers on one prestigious platform. Guided by the principles of halal, ethical trade, and sustainability, the Expo reflects a shared vision of inclusive growth and responsible commerce.

Held at the China–Bangladesh Friendship Expo Center, Dhaka from May 7–9, 2026, the event highlights Bangladesh’s growing role as an emerging hub in the multi-billion-dollar halal market. By connecting Bangladeshi production strength with a global halal consumer base of over two billion people, the Expo fosters meaningful partnerships across food, pharmaceuticals, cosmetics, textiles, tourism, and finance.

Beyond trade, Bangladesh Halal Expo 2026 celebrates innovation and lifestyle through dedicated thematic zones such as NOV (Natural, Organic & Vegan), Private Label Products, and SEFPEX (Aquaculture & Equipment)—creating a unified platform where tradition meets modern global demand.
